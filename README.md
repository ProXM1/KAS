🃏 Welcome to Mystic Hold'em
Mystic Hold'em is a magical, single-file browser adaptation of Texas Hold'em. Players compete against two AI opponents—Archmage Alaric and Rogue Sylas—in a battle of wits, luck, and mystical chip management. The objective is to win the pot by either forcing all other players to fold or by having the highest-valued hand at the final showdown.

📜 How to Play

You begin with a stack of 1,000 mystical coins (🪙). A hand consists of two private "hole" cards dealt to each player, and up to five "community" cards dealt face-up in the center of the table. You will use a combination of your hole cards and the community cards to form the strongest possible poker hand.

At the start of each hand, every player automatically contributes an ante/blind of 10 coins to seed the pot.

⚔️ The Flow of Battle

A single hand of Mystic Hold'em proceeds through the following phases:

Pre-Flop: Every player is dealt two private cards. A round of betting occurs based purely on the strength of these hidden cards.

The Flop: Three community cards are dealt face-up in the center of the magical felt. Another round of betting commences.

The Turn: A fourth community card is revealed, followed by a third round of betting.

The River: The fifth and final community card is revealed, triggering the final betting round.

The Showdown: If more than one player remains after the final round of betting, all cards are flipped. The player with the strongest hand wins the pot.

🪄 Player Actions

When it is your turn, the magical action bar will appear at the bottom of the screen. You must choose one of the following actions:

Fold: Surrender your cards and forfeit any coins you have already put into the pot. You will sit out until the next hand begins.

Call/Check: Match the current highest bet on the table to stay in the hand. If no one has raised the bet before you, this acts as a "Check," meaning you pass the action without putting in additional coins.

Raise: Increase the current bet. You must enter your desired raise amount into the input box. The other players must match your new total to remain in the game.

🏆 Winning the Pot

The game features a streamlined, custom hand evaluator designed for quick browser play. Hands are ranked primarily by finding duplicate values and suits. The general hierarchy of power is as follows:

Flush (Five cards of the same suit)

Four of a Kind (Quads)

Full House (Three of a kind + a pair)

Three of a Kind (Trips)

Two Pair

One Pair

High Card (If no player has a pair or better, the highest single card value wins)

Note: If all other opponents fold during any betting round, the remaining player automatically wins the pot without having to reveal their cards!
